The man behind Kronodigger - Alexey G, is a classically trained clarinetist, composer and audio engineer. Being highly influenced by electronic music since age 10, Alexey creates new works with a type of sound that never stays in one place. Driven by wild imagination, Kronodigger presents music with lots of sound design, sampling, automation and unexpected twists and turns. Kronodigger has no age restrictions, contraindications or side effects!
