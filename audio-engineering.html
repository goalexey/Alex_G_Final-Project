Alexey Gorokholinsky
Audio Engineering/
Video Production

Alexey Gorokholinsky has been working in the sphere of audio engineering for the past 10 years. Starting while still at school in Juilliard, he's been obsessively investing into new gear and software for best sound capture and processing results. In 2012 he became faculty at prestigious audio engineering school Institute of Audio Research, where he's been teaching classes on electronic instruments and acoustics. In 2013 he became the main audio engineer for classical music label LP Classics. This collaboration resulted in constant production of albums of many classical artists of New York area and abroad. He provides first class "on location" recording as he believes in importance of great space and instruments. For examples of his work, please check out the Mastering Portfolio page.

Video Production

For years, Alexey has been working as part of video production company PopovMedia. The company provides highest quality video recording, streaming, post production, and promo video creation. It has worked with such organizations as Jazz at Lincoln Center, Metropolitan Opera, The Juilliard School, Martha Graham Company, Opera America, Alvin Ailey, Sidra Bell Dance New York, and many other arts related organizations.
For rates and services, please Contact us directly with your inquiries.
